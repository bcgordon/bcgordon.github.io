✦ The story

Four pillars,
one place.

🌾

These apps didn't start as a suite — they started as personal problems that needed personal solutions. Each one grew out of a real friction point: a day that felt chaotic without a shape, an evening that ended with too much of the wrong thing, a conversation where listening slipped away.

The Plan came first — a quiet place to decide what today actually means before it gets away. Not a to-do list for productivity's sake, but a gentle anchor for the day ahead.

Find Enough grew from noticing that the things worth doing are easy to overdo. The goal isn't less joy — it's sustainable joy. A way of saying "that was good" and meaning it.

Pause is the most personal of the three. Built to support an ADHD mind that genuinely wants to listen, to follow others' leads, to be present — and sometimes just needs a little scaffolding to get there.

Letters from BC Gordon is something a little different — not a tool, but a conversation. An honest account of one person trying to loosen technology's grip on daily life. To spend less, own more of his own attention, and find a better balance between the screen and the world beyond it.

A meadow isn't manicured. It grows a little wild, a little different each season. These tools — and these letters — are the same. Works in progress, built with honesty, tended with care. Welcome.
